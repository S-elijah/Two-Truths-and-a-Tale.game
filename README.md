# 🎭 Two Truths and a Tale

**Two Truths and a Tale** is an interactive web game designed to help people understand each other on a deeper level. Through a series of thoughtful questions, players reflect on their inner world and then share their answers with a friend or partner via WhatsApp. It’s a modern take on classic icebreakers—blending storytelling, vulnerability, and connection.

## ✨ Features

- **10 Deeply Personal Questions** – Carefully crafted prompts that go beyond small talk.
- **Progress Tracking** – Visual progress bar and question counter to keep you oriented.
- **Smooth Navigation** – Go back or forward; your answers are saved automatically.
- **Beautiful Summary** – At the end, all answers are compiled into a neat, shareable format.
- **WhatsApp Integration** – Send your summary directly to any WhatsApp number with one click.
- **Copy to Clipboard** – Easily copy your story to use elsewhere.
- **Play Again** – Restart the journey anytime to answer anew or share with someone else.
- **Responsive Design** – Works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Open the game** in any modern web browser.
2. **Answer each question** honestly (or with a creative tale). The text area expands as you type.
3. **Click “Next”** to move forward. You can also go back using the “Back” button.
4. After answering all 10 questions, you’ll see a **summary** of your responses.
5. **Share your story**:
   - Enter a recipient’s WhatsApp number (with country code, e.g., `+1234567890`).
   - Click the green **Send** button to open WhatsApp with the pre‑filled message.
   - Alternatively, click **Copy summary** to paste it anywhere.
6. To start over, click **Play again** – perfect for sharing with another person or answering differently.

## 🛠️ Technical Details

- **Pure HTML / CSS / JavaScript** – No external dependencies except Font Awesome icons and Google Fonts.
- **Local storage** – Not used intentionally; all data stays in the session only.
- **WhatsApp link** – Uses the `wa.me` API to open a pre‑composed message.
- **Fully responsive** – Adapts to any screen size.
